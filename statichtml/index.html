Music is freedom

Before I was a Top 16 contestant on Season 4 of The Voice, I'd been really kept to myself about my music. I never wanted to be a superstar, I just wanted those with willing ears to listen. Music is such an amazing outlet. I'm not the best at speaking my heart, but through music, I am free. If you have willing ears, I encourage you to take a minute to listen to my music.

 
 

Money Tree

There are going to be times where you are the only one who thinks something is right. But, if you feel strongly enough about something, it will always pay off to follow your gut. This was my inspiration behind the song Money Tree.
